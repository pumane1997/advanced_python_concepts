Welcome to Flatmates Bill Sharing App!

Managing shared expenses has never been easier! Flatmates Bill is your ultimate solution for splitting bills seamlessly between two flatmates. Whether it's rent, utilities, or any shared household expense, this app simplifies the process and ensures fairness.

What We Offer:

  • Accurate Calculations: Enter the total bill amount and the number of days each flatmate stayed during the period. The app will calculate how much each person owes based on their share.
  • Detailed Reports: Generate a professional PDF report with the names of the flatmates, the billing period, and the precise amount each person has to pay.
  • Easy and Intuitive Interface: Designed to make bill-sharing stress-free, so you can focus on living harmoniously with your flatmate.

Say goodbye to awkward money conversations and let Flatmates Bill handle the math for you. Get started today!
